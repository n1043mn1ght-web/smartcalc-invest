Wealth Forecasting FAQ

The secret to Passive Income is time and compounding. Our engine simulates long-term market growth with monthly contribution logic.

What is the Rule of 72? A quick way to estimate when your money will double: divide 72 by your annual return rate.

Why are monthly contributions important? Regular deposits lower your risk through dollar-cost averaging and significantly fuel the Wealth Snowball.

What return rate should I use? Historical S&P 500 returns are around 8-10% annually (adjusted for inflation), but always plan with a conservative 6-7% for safety.
